The problem we discovered

Many restaurant goers frequently order takeout or visit for social occasions. However, many often experience indecisiveness, and over-complicated research while looking for ways to accommodate their budget and dietary needs. How might we improve restaurant goers' decision-making process to be more decisive by providing recommendations based on their dietary needs, budgets and proximity to ensure user satisfaction

Media object
The low fidelity sketch aimed to reflect the user flow developed as well as the potential screens for an brief app tour. However upon further iteration you’ll notice that we didn’t move forward with the app tour as we used the coaching screens as a guide.

Solution

A gamified way to combat indecisiveness that provides custom preferences for those looking to grab a bite on a discount.
